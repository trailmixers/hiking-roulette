Trail mixer




Our Goal

Sometimes it's hard to decide which hike you want to go on. Let us make that easy for you.

  1. Pick the Location where you want to hike.
  2. Choose the distance you want to hike (in miles).
  3. Pick the elevation gain (in feet) you want your hike to have. Take into account the following:
    • A larger elevation gain generally means a more difficult trail
    • Short distance + large elevation gain = more difficult "leg burner" hike
    • Longer distance + large elevation gain = gradual uphill hike
  4. Press the "Take Me Hiking" button and let us find your next trail and get outdoors quicker!

Trails!

Our Mission

It's 2015, the intersection between technology and the outdoors is getting busier and busier, and, incredibly, hikers of all skill levels are still faced with the difficult decision of deciding the right trail to take. Trail Mix aims to make your's life a little easier and get you outdoors quicker by sorting through libraries of hikes and randomly picking your ideal trail based on your input.
